Historical Attractions

Bunkart 1

Located in the outer circle of Tirana in the Communist Bunker. This museum shows how Albania was under the communist regime. It explores the conditions ordinary people lived in and the conditions the leaders lived in.

Bunkart 2

This bunker, located in the heart of Tirana by the City Hall explores briefly Albania under the communist regime. Suggested for those that cannot visit the original bunk'art. This bunker is the former anti-nuclear bunker for the Albanian Interior Ministry

National Museum

The national museum of Albania, located in the heart of Tirana by the City center. In here you can explore Albanian history dating back to the Skanderberg era but not only!

House of Leaves

Museum of secret surveillence in Albania during the communist regime. Explores the ways that communist exploited their power and citizens. There are also some interactive activities to keep audiences engaged!

Tirana Castle

An amazing "Pedonale" to visit or hang out with your friends in. You can find many restaurants, bars and shops inside of it.


Other Attractions

Tirana East Gate Mall (TEG)

Tirana's biggest mall. Big varity of shops ranging from clothing stores, technology stores, food courts and cinemas.

Toptani Shopping Center

If you have some time to kill, and are near the city center, then you should visit Toptani Shopping center. You can find many shops, a big food court and fun activities for young ages.

Salt Restaurant

If you are an undertermined person, this restaurant is for you. It offers a range of different foods including sushi, steak and European cuisine.

Artigiano Restaurant

An traditional Italian restaurant with an Albanian touch. There are a few different locations and they offer an amazing atmosphere.
