Top Attractions

Orikum

An Amazing area located near the main city of Vlora. If you like places which are calm during the day and buzz during the evenings, this is the place for you!

Drymades

An amazing nearby "village" in the country of Vlora. Covered by breathtaking resorts and hotels and clear blue rocky beaches, this is the perfect place for a summer vacation

Lungomare

For those who enjoy long walks along the sea, this is the perfect place for you. Along the road you can also find coffee, ice cream and many other shops!

Llogara Mountain

An amazing mountain covered by nature. Near the top you can also visit the Llogara National Park or rest at the restaurants in the nature with an indescribable view.


Historical Attractions

Independence Museum of Albania

Important piece of history for Albania. This museum in-depth explores Albania's Independence and the history behind it.

Vlora Old Town

An amazing part of Vlora which brings to light breathtaking architecture and views. Many places including traditional restaurants.

Independence statue

This Monument, located by Flag's Plaza, is an amazing sculpture created for the Declaration of Independence in Albania.

Kaninë Castle

This Medivial castle, located on top of a hill, offers an amazing view of Medivial times in Albania and Vlora City.

Muradie Mosque

An Ottoman mosque built in the 16th century, located by the Independence Museum of Vlora.


Other Attractions

Pirate's Cave

Breathtaking attraction you can visit by boat, sorrounded by clear blue water it is a must visit place.

Karaburun Peninsula

One of the best coasts in Albania, offering clear blue water, rocky beaches and nature all around. If you want to escape into a quite, full of nature place this is it!

Sazan Island

Albania's biggest island, which used to be a submarine base for the Soviets, now is a national marine park, with beautiful nature, and an interesting past, this is a must island near Vlora.

Saranda

One of the most visited coastal cities of Albania located in Vlora County. A fan-favourite among tourists and locals with many activities to do.

Blue Eye

Natural Mountain Spring with amazing clear blue water that you can also see nearly 50 meters deep into the spring.
