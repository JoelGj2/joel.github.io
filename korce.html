Welcome to Korçë

The bazaar of serenates is waiting for you! Explore the history, traditions and culture of this Southeastern Albania City.

Top Attractions

Lake Ohrid

The oldest lake in Europe, shared amongs the borders of North Macedonia and Albania. An amazing view with clear waters. Estimated to date back around 2-5 million years.

Voskopojë Village

An unqiue village that is part of the Korça County. It's magnificient history incliuding the 24 churches make it one of the most beautiful villages in Albania.

Pogradec

An amazing city in the county of Korça filled with history and breathtaking views, especially during the winter time.

Morava Mountain

A great mountain for those who enjoy outdoor activities. You can go for a hike


Historical Attractions

Mirahori Mosque

One of Albania's Oldest Mosques, built around the 15th century it has many stories to tell!

Resurrection of Christ Orthodox Cathedral

A breathtaking Orthodox church located in the middle of the city, it's architecture is what makes it so beautiful. The main Orthodox Church in Korçë.

Old Bazaar

Arguably, one of the most beautiful places in Korçë, a restored Ottoman-style Bazaar filled with coffee's, traditional shops and restaurants!

First Albanian School

An important piece of history, the first ever school based in the Albanian Language, where the alphabet was created. Must visit place to learn more about Albanian Language over the years.

National Museum of Medieval Art

Museum based on the Medieval art and history of Korçë. Located in the inner city, History lovers must visit.


Other Attractions & Activities

Lake Prespa

A peaceful lake which borders 3 countries, Albania, Macedonia and Greece. The perfect escape from the busy cities into nature.

Boboshtica Village

Amazing village near the Korça County known for it's traditional architecture and beautiful hiking trails.

Drenova National Park

If you are an Nature lover, then visiting the Drenova National Park is for you, the perfect nature escape filled with countless hiking trails, waterfalls and wildlife.

Winter Sports in Dardhë

For those who enjoy skiing or snowboarding, then Dardhë is a must visit place in Korçë. One of the most best places to enjoy these sports in Albania.

Korça Beer Festival

If you come to Korça in August, then don't forget to drop by the Beer Festival, one of the most known festivals in Albania. Perfect place to meet new people and drink lot's of beer!
