Top Attractions

Ali Pasha Bridge

An amazing bridge located a 30 minute hike away from Gjirokastër. Sorrounded by Nature, it is a great escape from the busy city.

Antigonea Archaeological Park

An amazing Heritage built in the 3rd Century BC. This enourmous and historical park offer a surreal adventure for those who explore it!

Exploring the Old Bazaar

Enjoying the city from within is just as good, if you prefer taking a short walk and enjoying local culture, exploring the old Bazaar is perfect for you!

Gjirokastër Castle

The most important historical place of Gjirokastër, with it's location on top of the entire city, it offers amazing views.


Historical Attractions

Ethnographic Museum

This Museum shows a cruical part of history, located in Albania's last communist leader birth house, Enver Hoxha, it is an iconic museum which talks about local culture.

Skenduli House Museum

Amazing preserved house from the Ottoman Empire-era. Hard work has gone towards preserving all furniture and architecture as it was.

Cold War Tunnel

This is an Emergency shelter built under the leadership of Enver Hoxha, which reflects just how paranoid he was.

Bazaar Mosque

A part of the few remaining Mosque's from the Ottoman Empire-era. Located by the Old Bazaar it is a must visit place for all tourist which want to learn more about the affect of them in Albania.


Other Attractions & Activities

Viroi Lake

A small lake located near Gjirokastër, but do not underestimate visiting it, especially in the Winter where you can see the mountains full of snow!

Trying Traditional and Local Foods

If you enjoy exploring local cuisine of places you visit, then you must try the local food such as 'Byreku Qahi' (in the picture), 'Qifqi', 'Lakrok me Thembër' and 'Hoshaf'

Trip to Përmet

An amazing local place to visit is Përmet, with it's Thermal Baths of Bënjë and Vjosa River.
